Members

isWild

Whether this bag is wild or not. When a bag is wild, any tile taken from in the bag can be used as any other tile. This is used client-side, where we are only concerned about the number of tiles left in the bag and we want to prevent reverse engineering of bag and rack contents. By default, bags are not wild.
