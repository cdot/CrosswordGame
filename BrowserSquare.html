$placeTile($tdnullable)

A tile has been placed on this square and we have to adjust the UI accordingly. This is normally invoked in response to a PLACE_TILE event, though may also be called directly during board and rack creation. The tile is assumed to already be attached to the Square.

$unplaceTile(tilenullable, $tdnullable)

Remove the currently placed tile's UI from the square's UI. This is normally invoked in response to an UNPLACE_TILE event, though may also be called directly during board and rack creation. By the time this is called, the tile has already been removed from the model of the square, we just need to detach the UI.

Parameters:
