Module: dawg/compressor

Command-line program to generate a DAWG (Directed Acyclic Word Graph) from a word lexicon. Generates a somewhat optimised Trie, encodes it in an integer array, which it then gzips.

Based on Appel & Jacobsen, with ideas from Weck and Toal. Not the fastest, or the most efficient, but who cares? It works.

node js/dawg/compressor.js will tell you how to use it.
