Class: Turn

Turn(game, params)

Communicating the result of a command from server back to client, and in a game history. Despite the name, a Turn is used not just for a player's turn (such as a play or a swap) but also for other results from commands sent to the server, such as challenges.

type :string

The 'type' of the turn. This will be one of

  • move: a player has made a move
  • swap: a player has execute a tile swap
  • Game over: the end of the game has been confirmed
  • challenge-failed: a challenge was not successful
  • challenge-won: a challenge succeeded
  • took-back: the last player took back their turn
